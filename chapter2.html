Chapter Two ❤️

Our First Date

4 February ❤️

I still remember how nervous I was before our first date.

I kept wondering if everything would go well.

Then I saw you...

The nerves disappeared.

All I could think about was how beautiful you looked.

We laughed together.

We spoke for hours.

Time went by so quickly because being with you felt so easy.

That day wasn't just our first date.

It was the beginning of countless memories that I can't wait to keep making with you.

"The best decision I ever made was asking you on that first date."
