Chapter Four ❤️

Our Forever

The Life I Dream About ❤️

When I think about the future... I don't think about places. I don't think about things. I think about you.

I dream about the day I finally get to call you my wife. Standing beside you during our nikah. Looking into your eyes and thanking Allah for bringing you into my life.

🤍 A beautiful nikah at home surrounded by our families.

🏡 A warm home filled with love and laughter.

☕ A little coffee corner where every morning starts with you.

📚 A bookshelf because you were reading a book the first time we spoke.

🍳 Cooking together in our own kitchen.

🎬 Movie nights on the couch with popcorn.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Raising our two children with love, kindness and Islam.

🕋 Making Umrah together and visiting Makkah and Madinah.

🌍 Travelling to Cape Town, the Drakensberg, Mauritius and Liverpool together.

🌅 Growing old while sitting on the porch watching the sunset hand in hand.

I know life won't always be easy. We'll have good days and difficult days. But as long as we're together... I know we'll get through everything.

You aren't just part of my future. You are my future.

"My favourite place will always be wherever you are. ❤️"
