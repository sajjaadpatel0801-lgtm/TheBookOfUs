Our First Anniversary

14 December 2024

One year together. A whole year of memories, laughter, love and growing together.

We went to Rosebank and spent the day enjoying every moment together.

We played arcade games and that was the day I saw your competitive side. You wanted to win, and watching you laugh and play made me love you even more.

Then we watched not one, not two, but three movies together. It felt like time had stopped because being with you was all I wanted.

And of course, we ended the day with Mochachos. It wasn't just the food that made it special—it was sharing that moment with you.

"I'd relive that day a thousand times if it meant being with you again."
