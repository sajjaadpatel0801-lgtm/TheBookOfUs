Chapter Three ❤️

Our First Anniversary

14 December 2024 ❤️

One whole year.

It honestly didn't feel real.

Twelve months of memories, laughs, lessons and growing together.

We spent our day at Rosebank.

We played so many games together.

That was the day I saw your competitive side, and I loved every second of it.

Then we watched a 3D movie together.

I don't remember every scene from the movie.

But I'll always remember sitting next to you, smiling at you, and thinking how lucky I was to call you mine.

After that we went to Mochachos and ended the day together.

It wasn't about where we went.

It was about who I was with.

Looking back now, that day became one of my favourite memories.

And one day, In Sha Allah, we'll look back at this anniversary while celebrating our fiftieth together.

"One year down... forever to go. ❤️"
