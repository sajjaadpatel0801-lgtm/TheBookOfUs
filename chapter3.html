Our First Anniversary

14 December 2024 ❤️

One whole year together. One whole year of loving you. One whole year of making memories that I will never forget.

We went to Rosebank. We laughed while playing all the different arcade games. That was the day I got to see your competitive side and I loved every second of it.

After all the games we watched not one... not two... but three movies together. I didn't care what was playing. Being next to you was already my favourite part.

Then we ended our perfect day with Mochachos. Simple moments... but they became memories that will stay with me forever.

"That wasn't just our first anniversary... it was one of the happiest days of my life."
