How We Met

It all started in Grade 10.

The first time I saw you, you were reading a book. I still remember that moment so clearly. I never imagined that one day the girl with the book would become the love of my life.

As the years went by, we got closer. Every conversation, every laugh and every memory slowly became another page in our story.

Looking back now, I know that meeting you was the best thing that has ever happened to me.
