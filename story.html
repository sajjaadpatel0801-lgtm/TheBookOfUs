It All Started...

It all started in Grade 10.

The first time I saw you, you were reading a book.

At that moment I didn't know your name. I didn't know your story. I didn't know that one day you would become the most important person in my life.

Looking back now... I realise that was the beginning of the greatest story I will ever live.

"The girl with the book became my whole world."
