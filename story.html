How We Met ❤️

It all started in Grade 10. The first time I saw you, you were standing in the hallway reading a book. You looked up. Our eyes met. You smiled. That tiny moment changed my whole life. I didn't know it then, but I had just met the girl I would spend the rest of my life loving. For years we only crossed paths. Then came Matric Ball... The night that changed everything.
