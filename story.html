Chapter One ❤️

Where It All Began...

It All Started...

It all started in Grade 10.

I already knew your name.

And if I'm being honest...

I had liked you for a long time.

Then one day...

I saw you sitting there reading your book.

That wasn't the first time I had seen you.

But it was the first time we really spoke.

I remember feeling nervous.

I kept wondering what I should say.

But the moment we started talking...

Everything just felt so natural.

I didn't know that one simple conversation would change my life forever.

I didn't know that the girl I had admired for so long would become my best friend...

The love of my life...

And one day...

My future wife.

Looking back now...

I realise that wasn't just the first conversation we ever had.

It was the first page of the greatest love story I will ever get to live.

And if I could go back to that very moment...

I would still walk over to you.

I would still start that conversation.

And I would still fall in love with you all over again. ❤️

"Every beautiful love story begins with one simple conversation."
